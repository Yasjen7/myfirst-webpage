Hi, I’m Yasmeen Vargas, a creative artist and IT student from NYC with a passion for blending the worlds of technology and art in a new way. My work spans digital art, painting, and film, and last year I’ve ventured into the complex field of cybersecurity, where I aim to merge my creativity with tech innovation.

ABOUT ME

I am a 25-year-old artist, born and raised in the Bronx, and a proud mom to my amazing 3-year-old son. I am passionate about community building that encourages better ways of living. With a deep curiosity for both art and technology, I’m driven to explore how they can coexist to uplift us and future generations. One of the reasons I decided to learn more about technology is because it plays such a major role in our daily lives. My first step into the tech world was through a cybersecurity bootcamp, and ever since, I’ve never looked back—nor do I want to. I’m passionate about educating others on essential tech hygiene practices to help protect their devices and data, especially when it comes to children. Watching my son effortlessly navigate technology has been a powerful reminder of how important it is to instill these habits early on. I’m always eager to learn, grow, and create spaces that inspire positive change.
